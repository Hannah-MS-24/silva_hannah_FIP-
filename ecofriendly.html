Summary of eco friendly

Sustainability Highlights

  • Algonquin Spa
  • 100% Organic and Solar Powered
  • Organic towels and linens: Dream Designs
  • Eminence Brand skincare products (100% organic)
  • Spa powered entirely by on-site solar panels
  • Wool carpets and bamboo flooring (renewable resources)
  • Resort Operations
  • Electric Fleet: 8 electric golf carts for staff services
  • Firewood Usage: Carbon-neutral heating in 60 fireplaces.

Bear Trail Dining Room

  • Organic vegetables grown on-site;
  • Composting of all kitchen waste for gardens;
  • Pesticide-free vegetable and flower gardens.

Energy Efficiency

  • LED and compact fluorescent lighting used extensively;
  • Transition to 4-stroke motors (cleaner than 2-stroke engines).

Recycling Efforts

  • Township recycles metals, paints, and batteries on special days;
  • Bottles collected from rooms for refund deposits.

Eco-Friendly Investments (2010/2021)

Recent Activities:

  • 2021: New 3,200 sq. ft. eco-staff housing (R30 walls, R60 ceiling);
  • 2020: Energy-efficient walk-in freezer and fridge.
  • 2017: Tesla and electric car charging stations for guests;
  • 2015: Carbon-neutral Adventure Lodge with wood-fired boilers;
  • 2013: Eco-staff housing (2 buildings, LED lights, R60 insulation);
  • 2010: Solar panels offset hot water in luxury suites and saltwater pool.

Land Stewardship

  • Township recycles metals, paints, and batteries on special days;
  • Bottles collected from rooms for refund deposits.

Other Improvements

  • Recycled driveway materials during repaving projects;
  • Wool carpets in dining rooms and guest suites;
  • Bamboo flooring in cabins and spa.
